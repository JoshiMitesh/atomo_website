Matter

"The Future of Smart Home Connectivity"

A universal standard that connects all your smart home devices securely, seamlessly, and effortlessly.

Universally compatible across brands and ecosystems—including Apple, Google, and Amazon.

Fast & Reliable local, low latency performance ensures instant response time.

Built-in security with end-to-end encryption ensures your data stays private and protected.

Effortless setup—just scan, connect, and take control of all your devices with ease.

Optimized for Matter Protocol Development

Fully compatible with ESP-IDF + Matter SDK
Ideal for developing secure, interoperable smart home and industrial Matter-compliant devices

ESP32-WROOM Module

Dual-core Xtensa LX6 CPU up to 240 MHz
Integrated Wi-Fi (2.4 GHz) and Bluetooth LE for Thread Border Router pairing or multi-protocol Matter support

Symmetrical I/O Layout with Dedicated Power Rails

Each GPIO pin is paired with its own VCC and GND for plug-and-play sensor/actuator integration
Designed for clean prototyping and easy interfacing with Matter-compliant peripherals

Stable Power Management

F5V USB input with onboard regulator to 3.3V
Clean power rails for noise-sensitive Matter devices

Integrated USB-to-Serial Interface

Plug-and-play programming with CP2102/CH340
Supports OTA and secure flashing for Matter firmware

Developer-Friendly Features

Reset and boot buttons for firmware upload
Status LEDs for power and GPIO activity monitoring
ESD and surge protection for field reliability

Applications

Smart plugs, lighting controllers, sensors, bridges, and border routers
Ideal for Matter ecosystem prototyping in smart homes and connected industry solutions
